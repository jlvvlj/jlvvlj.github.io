Platform

We build predictive solutions for biological systems, from complex disease models to therapeuthic targets and optimized de novo molecules.

Multi-Omics Disease Models

We relate multi-omics data to phenotypes and create highly accurate models of disease and healthy baselines, at the molecular, cell and tissue levels.
Deep Mechanisms understanding

Poorly understood mechanisms of action lead to late stage failures. Combined network theory and deep learning helps infer underlying mechanisms.
Target identification

Automated graph reasoning and predictive inverse binding helps identify and evaluate non-obvious targets and de-orphan efficient molecules.
Hit / Lead Generation

We combine fast database multi-criteria screening to Deep generative models to evaluate or create pre-optimized therapeuthic molecules.
Hypothesis suggestion

Automated reasoning over biological models allows us to suggest the most direct path to scientific proof. Our generator suggests the best hypothesis to be tested in vitro.

Patients sub-groups identification

Our technology is based on heterogeneity. We measure variations between healthy and disease biological systems as well as between patient groups.
